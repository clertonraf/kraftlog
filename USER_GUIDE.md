# Quick Start Guide - Workout Logging

## How to Log a Workout

### Step 1: Navigate to Your Routine
1. Open the app and go to the **Routines** tab
2. Tap on your active routine to view details
3. Tap the **"Start Workout"** button

### Step 2: Select a Workout
1. You'll see a list of all workouts in your routine
2. Each workout shows:
   - Order number
   - Workout name
   - Number of exercises
   - Recommended rest interval
3. Tap the **play button** (â–¶) on any workout to begin

### Step 3: Log Your Sets
1. The app shows your current exercise with:
   - Exercise name
   - Recommended sets and reps (if available)
   - Progress indicator showing which exercise you're on

2. To log a set:
   - Tap **"Add Set"**
   - Enter the number of **repetitions** you completed
   - Enter the **weight in kg** you used
   - Tap **"Save"**

3. The set will appear in your list with a green checkmark

4. Repeat for all sets of the current exercise

### Step 4: Navigate Between Exercises
- Tap **"Next"** to move to the next exercise
- Tap **"Previous"** to go back to a previous exercise
- The progress bar at the top shows your position

### Step 5: Complete Your Workout
1. After logging all exercises, tap **"Complete Workout"**
2. Confirm the completion
3. Your workout is now saved to history!

## Troubleshooting

### "Failed to start workout"
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure you're logged in
- Try refreshing the routine list

### Missing exercise names
- This shouldn't happen anymore, but if it does:
- Contact support with the workout ID
- Exercise data might need to be updated in the system

### Can't complete workout
- Make sure you've logged at least one set
- Check that you have internet connection
- Try again after a few seconds

### History not showing
- Pull down to refresh the list
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure you've completed at least one workout
